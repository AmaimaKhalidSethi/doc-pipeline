Vendor Services Agreement Summary

Parties: Brightline Logistics Inc. ("Vendor") and Cascade Foods Co. ("Client")
Effective Date: 2026-08-01
Term: 12 months, automatically renewing unless either party gives 60 days' notice.

Scope: Vendor will provide cold-chain freight services for Client's Pacific Northwest distribution centers, including the Seattle, Portland, and Boise facilities.

Key obligations:
- Vendor must maintain temperature logs for all shipments and deliver them to Client's compliance team within 24 hours of delivery.
- Client must submit weekly shipment forecasts to Vendor every Friday by 5pm.
- Payment terms are net-30 from invoice date.
- Either party may terminate for cause with 15 days written notice if the other party fails to cure a material breach.
